My Bio

I was born and raised in Salt Lake City. I went to college at Westminster and I studied fine art. After graduation, I decided to move to Seattle, where I would love to end up again one day.

In Seattle, I worked as an AmeriCorps member for social service agencies. My favorite thing was teaching art classes to teenagers in residential treatment. When I moved back to Utah, I continued to work in the nonprofit world. Eventually I decided to explore graphic design as a career and worked for a while designing wedding invitations. After that, I landed at my current job, which involves design and general marketing. After taking an introductory HTML/CSS couse through the U, I decided to explore coding.

When I'm not working, I'm working on side art projects, hiking, gardening, reading, going to the movies, or spending time with friends and family.
